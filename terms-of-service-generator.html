Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really need terms of service for my website?

Yes. Terms of service protect your business by establishing the rules and guidelines users must agree to when using your website or app. They help limit your liability, protect your intellectual property, define acceptable use, and establish governing law for disputes.

Is this terms of service generator free to use?

Yes, completely free. No sign-up, no email required, and no premium paywalls. Unlike Termly or PrivacyPolicies.com, every feature is available at no cost. The tool runs entirely in your browser so your data stays private.

What's the difference between terms of service and terms and conditions?

Terms of service (TOS) and terms and conditions (T&C) are essentially the same thing -- a legal agreement between you and your users. The names are interchangeable. Some sites also call them "terms of use." Our generator creates a document that works regardless of what you choose to call it.

Can I customize the generated terms of service?

Absolutely. Our generator lets you customize clauses for your specific needs including user accounts, intellectual property, payment terms, liability limitations, and governing jurisdiction. You can also edit the output directly before copying it.

Are the generated terms of service legally binding?

Our generator produces professional terms of service based on widely-used legal templates. For the terms to be enforceable, users must agree to them (typically via a clickwrap or browsewrap agreement). We recommend having a lawyer review the final document to ensure it meets your jurisdiction's requirements.
