Frequently Asked Questions

What is a cookie policy and do I need one?

A cookie policy is a document that discloses what cookies your website uses, why they are used, and how users can control them. Under GDPR, the ePrivacy Directive, and similar laws worldwide, any website that uses cookies (including analytics, ads, or session cookies) must inform users and obtain consent. If your site uses cookies, you need a cookie policy.

Is this cookie policy generator GDPR compliant?

Yes. Our generator produces cookie policies that address GDPR requirements including cookie categorization, purpose disclosure, third-party cookie identification, consent mechanisms, and user rights. The generated policy covers the key elements required by the EU's ePrivacy Directive and GDPR.
